META BSE Role Overview

As a Business Support Engineer at META, you'll:

  • Help partners integrate META's APIs (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Troubleshoot technical issues in real-time
  • Work with cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Monitor and maintain partner integrations
  • Communicate technical concepts to diverse audiences
  • Manage multiple concurrent projects
  • Provide 24/7 on-call support (via rotation)
